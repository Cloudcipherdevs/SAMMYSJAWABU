About Sammy's Jawabu Academy

At Sammy's Jawabu Academy, we believe in nurturing the whole child – academically, socially, and spiritually. Our dedicated staff, modern facilities, and comprehensive curriculum ensure that every student reaches their full potential.

Located in the serene environment of Rongai Constituency, Nakuru, our academy provides the ideal setting for learning and growth. We offer both Junior and Senior School programs following the Kenyan curriculum with a focus on holistic development.
